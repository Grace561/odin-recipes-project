Spaghetti and Egg,onions,Tomatoes

Ingredient

  • Spaghetti
  • Egg
  • Onions
  • Knorr Cube
  • Cooking oil
  • Tomatoes
  • Papper
  • salt

Step

  1. Parboil your spaghetti and set aside.
  2. Place a pot of oil on fire and allow to heat.
  3. Pour your diced tomatoes, pepper and onion at once and and fry for like 2mins .
  4. Add your seasoning cube. Whisk your eggs and pour in the pot.
  5. Immediately pour your already parboiled spaghetti and stir together.
  6. Add salt to taste.
  7. Your spaghetti is ready. Serve with any chilled soft drink of your choice.
